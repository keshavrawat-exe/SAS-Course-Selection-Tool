Course Selection Tool

Instructions

Welcome to the SAS Course Selection Tool, a website designed to help you with planning your course options for High School at SAS.
For the best experience, follow these instructions on how to use the site.

When choosing your courses, first choose the credit which it gives, and then choose the course within it.
Please note that some courses are listed under a certain academic area in the PPG, but actually give Elective credits. These courses will be found in the Elective credit section.
Grades 9 and 10 must have 7 credits, so be sure to input the details for all rows.
Grades 11 and 12 have the option of taking 6 credits. You can leave one row blank if you plan to take 6 credits

After you're done inputting your course choices for each grade, you can press Save. This will allow you to reload and your course choices will be saved for that year.
Please remember to save all grades' course choices before you reload or leave the site. When you have all the courses you need, press the calculate button at the bottom.
Then, look at the Things to Change section to find any revisions you need to make to your plan.

I hope you enjoy using this site, and that it makes your High School course planning experience easier and faster.
- Keshav
